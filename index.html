a new goal

Posted on 2016-02-27

eh, I am working on my bachelor thesis these days. The title is “Cluster ensembles algorithm and its application in cancer gene expression”. In the whole Feb, I celebrate Spring Festival with my family and learned a little ahout machine learning methods. And now, I know a little about clustering methods, mostly the simple ones like K-MEANS, Fuzzy-C-Means, KNN and so on. And many of them are learned in the pattern recogintion lessons last year. Thanks to teacher Deng. Still I know little about Ensemble Clusters, I only know that it’s a theory proposed by Strehl. And I need to read his papers to learn about this theory.

There are a lot of work and experiments for me. And I am playing Dota2 in the dormitory, damn it! I find that I have spent 1100 hours in this game through the 4-year undergraduate life. Working in my dormitory is always inefficient for me because I always think that the dormitory is a place to take a rest, and I do not work in the dormitory. Yesterday, I went to the library, I spent the whole day there and found out that I wasted another day there. I read no paper, I just read through all the information in my SNS acounts, weibo(like twitter in china), zhihu(China’s Quora). I always escape from my work because it’s complex and difficult. The inefficiency is not about the work place, it’s just my attitude towards works is wrong. I think it’s time to change myself and promote myself.

So I made a goal, I will work 7 hours everyday. From 9 am to 12 am, then have a rest to 1 p.m. and then work to 5 pm. I treat my resesarch as a work, because I always treat works seriously, and never do things that has no relations about it.

I will try to reach this goal since Feb 27th 2016, I may update my status.And may you have a blessed day, Immanuel.
