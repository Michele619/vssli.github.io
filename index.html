Long time no see

Posted on 2016-04-08

It’s been a long time since I update my blog last time. At Feb, I made a goal that I will work like about 7 hours a day. About that, I can say that I failed it. People are easy to fall into a circumstance that they made goals and fail to follow it and it’s like a circle, people just repeat it. Goals are often made at the beginning of something, like New year wishes, New semester goals. It is really a difficult thing to keep to your goals you set.

Eh, I do not mean to talk about goals and how people(including me) repeat their foolish behaviors in this post. I wanna say something about what happened these days, these several weeks that I do not post anything.

My roommate and friend Liu failed his APS (I do not know much about, It’s something that is needed for the Germany University’s admissions), So maybe he has to gap for one year. I was also thinking of gap for like one year, applying for RA of some colleges, publish several thesis on journal. In this way, I maybe admitted to better colleges.

Umass reject me. The comittee refused my application. I feel bad about it, “I regret to inform you, however, that they were unable to recommend your admission”. I hate to hear such words. I quiet like this univerisity, I am a little upset.

Another roommate of mine, Ren got admitted to the Graduate School of Wuhan University, School of Software. I am happy for him. Hope all my friends have a bright and successful future.

These days, I am working on my bachelor thesis, doing some works about Custer Ensemble. In this post, I wanna record one thing, which is, In matlab, the command “which -all kmeans” can help me figure out which kmeans function I am using. This command helped me out of an error of kmeans:)

That’s it. Hope everyone has a blessed day!

eh, Should I post some academic things in my blog? Not just little things about my life? I am considering about it.
